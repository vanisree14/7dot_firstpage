**Badge** — small uppercase status/label pill. Use for trip status, counts, "new"/"surprise" flags.

```jsx
<Badge tone="teal" dot>Surprise inside</Badge>
<Badge gradient>Featured</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning">2 seats left</Badge>
```
Tones: `brand` `teal` `neutral` `success` `warning` `danger`. Modifiers: `solid`, `gradient`, `dot`.
