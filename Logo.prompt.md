**Logo** — renders the official RV logo lockups from PNG assets. Choose `layout`, `tone`, and `tagline`; set `basePath` to where the logo PNGs live in your project.

```jsx
<Logo layout="horizontal" tone="gradient" height={44} basePath="assets/logos" />
<Logo layout="symbol" tone="white" height={32} />
<Logo layout="vertical" tone="white-on-color" tagline />
```
Use the gradient lockup on white, white/white-on-color on photos & gradient panels, black for single-color print. Never recolor or distort the artwork.
