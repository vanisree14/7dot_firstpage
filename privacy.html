Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Roverland Voyage ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we look after it. We keep it plain — no fine-print games.

Information we collect

  • Details you give us — name, phone, email and trip preferences when you enquire or book.
  • Usage data — basic, anonymised analytics about how our site is used, to make it better.
  • Communications — the messages you send us, so we can plan your voyage well.

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries and design, confirm and support your trip.
  • To send you updates you've asked for (such as new-voyage waitlists). You can opt out anytime.
  • To improve our website and services.

Sharing

We never sell your data. We share only what's necessary with trusted partners who help deliver your trip (accommodation, vehicle and local providers), and only to fulfil your booking.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct or delete your personal information at any time by emailing us. We'll act on reasonable requests promptly.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies for essential functionality and anonymised analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser; some features may be affected.
