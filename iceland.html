Day 1
Arrival — coastal charm & capital vibes

Land in Reykjavík and check into your premium hotel. Unwind, meet your fellow explorers, and toast the adventure ahead at a Welcome Gala Dinner and route briefing.

Day 2
Into the wild — Snæfellsnes Peninsula

Pick up your Land Rover Defender and roar through lava fields to the wild, windswept Snæfellsnes Peninsula. Sea cliffs at Svörtuloft, the Saxhóll crater, black beaches — and a night by the coastal charm of Arnarstapi.

Day 3
Whale whispers & the Golden Circle

Set sail into the North Atlantic for whale encounters, then cruise Iceland's legendary Golden Circle — Þingvellir National Park, a dinner inside a tomato greenhouse, and a night beside erupting geysers.

★ Whale encounters
Day 4
Gullfoss & the glacier

Stand at the edge of the thundering Gullfoss waterfall, then tear across Langjökull Glacier on a snowmobile. Drive to Hella for a peaceful night — and a possible Northern Lights display.

★ Aurora possible
Day 5
Skógafoss & Reynisfjara

Visit thunderous Skógafoss, secret waterfalls, and surreal black sand beaches. Watch the waves crash against the Reynisfjara coast before resting in the village of Vík.

Day 6
Vatnajökull, Glacier Lagoon & Diamond Beach

Walk Diamond Beach, where the ocean leaves ice like jewels on black sand, and cruise past sculpted icebergs on Jökulsárlón with Vatnajökull's white giants watching from above.

Day 7
ATV ride & the road home

Start the day with an exhilarating ATV ride across volcanic black sands, then drive back to Reykjavík and return your Defender after a week of exploration.

★ ATV on volcanic sands
Day 8
The Blue Lagoon

Relax in the Blue Lagoon's healing, milky-blue waters or explore the city at your own pace. Celebrate the journey with a fine-dining Icelandic farewell feast.

Day 9
Departure

Savor one last Icelandic breakfast and a final coastal view. Depart with a suitcase full of memories — and magic.
