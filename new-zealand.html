Trip Overview

A summer road trip you'll never stop telling people about

New Zealand's South Island is a highlight reel of a planet — glaciers and rainforest, fjords and turquoise lakes, all stitched together by some of the most beautiful driving roads on Earth. We've routed it for wonder, balanced the big drives with slow days, and saved the best surprise for the turn of the year. You bring the sense of adventure; we'll bring everything else.

What You'll Experience

The South Island's greatest hits

Southern Alps

Snow peaks mirrored in glacier lakes the colour of raw turquoise.

Milford Sound

A fjord so steep and still it rewrites your sense of scale.

Crown Range & Queenstown

The highest sealed road in NZ, dropping into adventure-town.

Lake Tekapo dark skies

A UNESCO Dark Sky Reserve — the Milky Way, unfiltered.

Fiordland & rainforest

Moss, mist and waterfalls that appear only when it rains.

West Coast glaciers

Franz Josef and Fox — ice rivers tumbling toward the sea.
